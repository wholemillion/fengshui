## The Ancient Wisdom of Chinese Zodiac

The Chinese Zodiac, known as Sheng Xiao (生肖), is a repeating 12-year cycle where each year is represented by an animal and its reputed attributes. This system has been an integral part of Chinese culture for over 2,000 years, influencing people's decisions about career, relationships, and major life events.
