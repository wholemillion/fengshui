## Welcome to the Year of the Snake 2025

2025 is the **Year of the Wood Snake** (乙巳年), beginning on January 29, 2025 and ending on February 16, 2026. The Snake is the sixth animal in the Chinese Zodiac, representing wisdom, intuition, and transformation.

The Wood element adds qualities of growth, creativity, and flexibility to the Snake's natural characteristics. This combination suggests a year of thoughtful progress, strategic planning, and personal transformation.

### What the Year of the Snake Brings

The Snake year is generally associated with:
- **Introspection and wisdom**
- **Strategic planning and careful action**
- **Transformation and shedding old patterns**
- **Financial opportunities through careful investment**
- **Deepening of relationships**
