## Understanding Feng Shui Fundamentals

Feng Shui, which translates to "wind-water" in English, is an ancient Chinese practice that dates back over 3,000 years. It's the art of arranging your environment to create harmony and balance, promoting positive energy flow (known as "Chi" or "Qi") throughout your living and working spaces.

In this section, you'll discover the core principles that form the foundation of Feng Shui practice:

- **What is Feng Shui** - The history and philosophy behind this ancient art
- **The Five Elements Theory** - Understanding Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water
- **Yin and Yang** - The concept of balance and duality
- **The Bagua Map** - A powerful tool for mapping energy in your space

Whether you're new to Feng Shui or looking to deepen your understanding, these fundamental concepts will help you create more harmonious environments and improve various aspects of your life.
