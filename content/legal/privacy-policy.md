## Cookies and Tracking Technologies

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device. We use cookies to:
- Remember your preferences
- Analyze website traffic
- Serve personalized advertisements

### Types of Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| Essential | Website functionality | Session |
| Analytics | Traffic analysis | 2 years |
| Advertising | Google AdSense | 1 year |

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.
