What is Feng Shui? Feng Shui (风水), pronounced “fung shway,” is an ancient Chinese practice that dates back over 3,000 years. The term translates to “wind-water” in English, representing two of the most fundamental elements in nature that carry and direct energy. At its core, Feng Shui is the art and science of arranging your environment to create harmony, balance, and positive energy flow.
