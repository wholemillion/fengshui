Bedroom Feng Shui: Create a Restful Sanctuary for Better Sleep and Romance

Transform your bedroom into a peaceful retreat with Feng Shui principles. Learn the best bed placement, colors, and decor tips for better sleep and enhanced relationships.
May 15, 2025 · 5 min

Why Bedroom Feng Shui Matters#

Your bedroom is one of the most important rooms in your home. It’s where you spend approximately one-third of your life sleeping, regenerating, and connecting with your partner. In Feng Shui, the bedroom directly influences your health, relationships, and overall well-being.

A well-designed bedroom should be a sanctuary—a place of rest, intimacy, and renewal. When the Feng Shui is balanced, you’ll experience better sleep, improved relationships, and a greater sense of peace.

The Command Position: The Most Important Rule#

What is the Command Position?#

The command position is the most important Feng Shui principle for bedroom furniture placement. Your bed should be positioned so that:

  1. You can see the bedroom door while lying in bed
  2. The bed is NOT directly in line with the door
  3. The bed has a solid wall behind it (not a window)

Why It Matters#

When you can see the door without being directly in its path, you feel:

  • Safe and secure - You’re not surprised by anyone entering
  • In control - You can see opportunities coming
  • Relaxed - Your nervous system can fully rest

What to Avoid#

❌ Bed directly facing the door - Chi rushes at you while sleeping ❌ Bed under a window - No support, energy escapes ❌ Bed with foot pointing directly at door - “Coffin position” ❌ Bed in line with bathroom door - Negative energy

Best Colors for Bedroom Feng Shui#

Recommended Colors#

The best bedroom colors are Yin colors that promote rest and relaxation:

Colors to Avoid#

Accent Colors#

You can add small amounts of Yang colors as accents:

  • Red or pink accents for romance
  • Gold accents for prosperity
  • Green plants for growth

Essential Bedroom Feng Shui Tips#

1. Clear Under the Bed#

Why: Energy needs to circulate around you while you sleep

What to do:

  • Keep the space under your bed clear
  • If you must store items, use soft, rounded containers
  • Avoid sharp-edged storage under the bed

2. Balance Both Sides#

Why: Creates equality in relationships

What to do:

  • Have matching nightstands on both sides
  • Equal lighting on each side
  • Equal access to the bed from both sides

3. Choose the Right Headboard#

Why: Provides support and stability

Best options:

  • Solid wood headboard (most supportive)
  • Upholstered headboard (comfortable)
  • Avoid open slats or bars

4. Remove Electronics#

Why: Electronics emit EMFs and distract from rest

What to do:

  • Keep TVs out of the bedroom
  • Charge phones in another room
  • Use a traditional alarm clock

5. Use Appropriate Artwork#

Why: Images affect your subconscious mind

Best choices:

  • Peaceful landscapes
  • Images of pairs (for relationships)
  • Art that makes you feel calm

Avoid:

  • Sad or lonely images
  • Single figures (if seeking relationship)
  • Violent or disturbing art

Feng Shui for Better Sleep#

Create a Sleep-Friendly Environment#

  1. Darkness - Use blackout curtains
  2. Quiet - Address noise issues
  3. Cool Temperature - 65-68°F (18-20°C) is ideal
  4. Fresh Air - Open windows during the day
  5. Clean Air - Use air purifier or plants

Bedding Recommendations#

  • Natural materials (cotton, linen, silk)
  • Comfortable mattress that supports your body
  • Clean, fresh sheets washed regularly
  • Appropriate weight for the season

Remove Sleep Disruptors#

  • Clutter near the bed
  • Mirrors facing the bed
  • Exercise equipment
  • Work-related items
  • Unfinished projects

Feng Shui for Romance and Relationships#

For Singles Seeking Love#

  1. Create space for a partner

    • Clear a drawer for someone else
    • Make room in your closet
    • Have two nightstands

  2. Use pairs

    • Two pillows
    • Two candles
    • Paired artwork

  3. Add romantic elements

    • Pink or rose colors
    • Rose quartz crystals
    • Fresh flowers

For Couples#

  1. Maintain balance

    • Equal space on both sides of the bed
    • Shared decision on decor
    • No reminders of past relationships

  2. Enhance connection

    • Artwork depicting pairs
    • Photos of happy moments together
    • Soft, romantic lighting

  3. Remove obstacles

    • TV out of the bedroom
    • Work items removed
    • Clear communication space

Common Bedroom Feng Shui Mistakes#

Mistake 1: Mirror Facing the Bed#

Problem: Mirrors reflect energy and can disturb sleep

Solution:

  • Cover mirrors at night
  • Move mirrors to not face the bed
  • Use mirrored closet doors only if necessary

Mistake 2: Bed Against a Wall#

Problem: One partner feels trapped; imbalance in relationship

Solution:

  • Leave space on both sides of the bed
  • If room is small, at least 18 inches on each side

Mistake 3: Too Many Electronics#

Problem: EMFs and blue light disrupt sleep

Solution:

  • Remove TV from bedroom
  • Keep phones away from bed
  • Use battery-powered alarm clock

Mistake 4: Clutter#

Problem: Stagnant energy affects sleep and mood

Solution:

  • Regularly declutter
  • Use storage solutions
  • Keep surfaces clear

Bedroom Feng Shui Checklist#

Use this checklist to optimize your bedroom:

  • Bed in command position
  • Solid headboard behind bed
  • Equal space on both sides of bed
  • Matching nightstands
  • Soft, restful colors
  • No mirrors facing the bed
  • Electronics minimized or removed
  • Clutter cleared
  • Space under bed clear
  • Artwork is peaceful and positive
  • Good quality mattress and bedding
  • Appropriate lighting (dimmable is best)
  • Windows have good curtains/blinds
  • Door can close completely

Conclusion#

Your bedroom should be your personal sanctuary—a place where you can fully rest, restore your energy, and connect with your partner. By applying these Feng Shui principles, you can create a space that supports better sleep, improved relationships, and overall well-being.

Start with the most important change: positioning your bed in the command position. Then, gradually implement other improvements as you’re able. Notice how small changes can make a big difference in how you feel in this important space.

Remember, the best bedroom Feng Shui is one that feels right to you. Use these guidelines as a starting point, and trust your intuition about what creates the most peaceful, restful environment for your needs.
